My Big 10 Year Goals

Personal Goal

Become an accomplished artist with a full portfolio of 20 pieces of artwork.

  • Continue to build the portfolio to create a collection of artworks intended to become a cherished legacy for family members.
  • Complete 3 medium scale paintings, one for each of my children, as a gift to pass on.
  • Paint a custom mural for a family member that may be interested in adding a unique, personalized touch to their home.
  • Compile a portfolio to showcase some of my favorite pieces.
  • Enroll in a local class every 3 months to further enhance my knowledge and skills.
  • Join a local artist club to learn from the experiences of other artists.
  • Create one piece of artwork each month using any medium desired.
  • Complete one training video each month on how to improve a skill.
  • Complete one training video each month on a new artistic skill.

Personal:

In the next 10 years, my objective is to become an accomplished artist, refining and expanding my artistic skills. My focus will be to create artwork for my family intended to enrich their lives for generations to come. My artistic passions include drawing, painting, sculpting, and photography, though this is not where they end. I wish to enrich myself and experiment with and learn many different types of artistic mediums, perhaps even create styles of my own. This personal goal aligns with many of my values. Life-long learning is a core value, and I will explore and experiment with new techniques and artistic mediums. Additionally, it aligns with my value for family, as I aspire to not only inspire them, but also create artworks that touch their hearts, leaving behind a legacy that can be passed down through generations. My aspirations for this goal are very personal. Public recognition is not my driving force, but instead I aspire to explore new artistic mediums and create meaningful artworks filled with emotional expression, and to capture and document my life.

The impact I wish to make is to inspire my family to embrace their own creativity and to cherish the time we all have together. With my art, I want to create tangible reminders of our relationships and to always remind them of how important they are to me. I want the artwork to serve as comforting pieces they can immerse themselves in throughout the different stages of life. This journey will also provide fulfillment with personal growth, as I will be able to gain insights into myself and emotions. The fulfillment will also come when emotional connections are strengthened through the artwork.

Professional Goal

Become a freelance programmer with a home office setup and proficiency in at least three programming languages.

  • Create a comprehensive portfolio featuring a selection of websites I have designed that includes detailed information on the design process and functionality.
  • Become proficient in CSS and JavaScript by taking online courses.
  • Complete bachelors degree in Graphic Information Technology.
  • Review and select a coding bootcamp with an excellent track record to further enhance my skills and knowledge.
  • Research and select another programming language to delve into.
  • Practice programming in CSS or JavaScript at least 30 minutes daily.
  • Design and create three different example websites to gain working experience.

Professional:

In my professional journey, I aim to become a freelance programmer that can offer services from the comfort of my home within the next 10 years. This will provide professional freedom and allow me to manage my own schedule. I enjoy the ever-changing nature of technology and the endless learning it provides. In the realm of my professional pursuits, programming and lifelong learning stand out as my primary passions. The ever-evolving nature of technology and the endless possibilities it presents fuel my curiosity and drive for continuous growth. As for which values align with this goal, freedom is a core value that resonates with me professionally as I wish to have the freedom to explore new ideas and create a life that aligns with my passions and aspirations. I value autonomy and dream of being able to set my own schedule and have flexibility to balance my personal and professional life. Additionally, creativity plays a role in my professional goal as aim to create my own software or game that will display my personal and professional skills. Furthermore, a core value is life-long learning as I always strive to expand my knowledge and skills.

My main aspiration is to continue to learn and improve my programming skills. I also wish to broaden my current skills and learn new programming languages as I progress. The impact I hope to make is on future clients, to create either a webpage, software, or application that can empower their businesses or services. Programming brings me joy as I find the challenge of problem solving to be invigorating. Furthermore, being capable of providing a wonderful product will be fulfilling knowing that I can share my skills and expertise with the clients.
